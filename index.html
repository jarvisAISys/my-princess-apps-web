Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how My Princess Apps ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the Princess Fashion Show app (the "App").

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The App does not require an account, login, or any personal details to use.

Data Storage

All data created within the App — including colored artwork and saved images — is stored locally on your device only. We have no access to this data and it is never transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Princess Fashion Show is designed for children. We are committed to protecting the privacy of children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected at all, the App is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

In-App Purchases

The App is completely free and contains no in-app purchases or advertisements.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
